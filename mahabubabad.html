Mahabubabad Attractions

Bayyaram Lake
A beautiful and serene lake surrounded by hills, Bayyaram Lake is a great spot for nature lovers and bird watchers. The lake is particularly stunning during the monsoon season when the surroundings are lush green.
Cost to Stay: ₹1,400/night
Kuravi Veerabhadra Swamy Temple
This ancient temple dedicated to Lord Veerabhadra, an incarnation of Lord Shiva, is an important pilgrimage site. The temple is surrounded by greenery, making it a peaceful place to visit.
Cost to Stay: ₹1,400/night
Edubadi Ramalingeswara Temple
A lesser-known yet architecturally significant temple, Edubadi Ramalingeswara is known for its intricate carvings and religious importance. It is a tranquil spot for both devotees and architecture enthusiasts.
Cost to Stay: ₹1,000/night
Pakala Lake
A natural lake surrounded by forests, Pakala Lake is a hidden gem for nature lovers. The peaceful surroundings and the abundant birdlife make it a perfect spot for a day out.
Cost to Stay: ₹1,200/night
