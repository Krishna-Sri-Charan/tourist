Rangareddy Attractions

Chilkur Balaji Temple
Popularly known as the "Visa Balaji Temple," this temple is famous among devotees who wish to travel abroad. The temple is set in a serene environment and is a significant pilgrimage spot.
Cost to Stay: ₹3,360/night
Gandipet Lake (Osman Sagar)
Gandipet Lake is a popular weekend destination for families, offering boating, picnics, and stunning sunset views. The area is surrounded by lush greenery and is a great spot for relaxation.
Cost to Stay: ₹1,963/night
Ocean Park
A fun amusement and water park located near Gandipet, Ocean Park is a popular spot for families and children. It offers various water rides, games, and entertainment options.
Cost to Stay: ₹1,963/night
