Mancherial Attractions

Sri Gudem Temple
Sri Gudem Temple is a renowned spiritual center, attracting devotees for its sacred atmosphere and ancient history. The temple holds special significance in the region, and festivals bring it to life with grand celebrations.
Cost to Stay: ₹2,126/night
Bellampalli Hills
Bellampalli Hills are perfect for those looking for a scenic hike. The hills offer panoramic views of the Mancherial landscape, making it a must-visit for trekking enthusiasts and photography lovers.
Cost to Stay: ₹2,400/night
Mandamarri Waterfalls
The Mandamarri Forest is a rich biodiversity hotspot, teeming with wildlife and lush greenery. It’s an ideal spot for nature walks, bird watching, and experiencing the serenity of the forest.
Cost to Stay: ₹2,400/night
