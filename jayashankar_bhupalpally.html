Jayashankar Bhupalpally Attractions

Eturnagaram Wildlife Sanctuary
One of the oldest sanctuaries in Telangana, Eturnagaram is home to several species of wildlife including tigers, leopards, and deer. The sanctuary is also known for its lush greenery and dense forests.
Cost to Stay: ₹3,000/night
Laknavaram Lake
A picturesque lake surrounded by hills and forests, Laknavaram is a popular spot for picnics and boating. The hanging bridge over the lake is a major attraction.
Cost to Stay: ₹1,600/night
