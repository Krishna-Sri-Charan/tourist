Rajanna Sircilla Attractions

Sri Raja Rajeshwara Swamy Temple
Another important temple in Sircilla, this temple is known for its beautiful architecture and spiritual significance. The temple complex also houses a large water tank.
Cost to Stay: ₹2,600/night
Sircilla Weaving Centre
Sircilla is known for its textile industry, particularly its weaving centers. Visitors can explore the local weaving techniques and purchase authentic handloom sarees.
Cost to Stay: ₹2,528/night
Maner River
The Maner River flows through Sircilla, offering scenic views and opportunities for boating and picnics. The riverbanks are ideal for a relaxing day out with family.
Cost to Stay: ₹1,400/night
