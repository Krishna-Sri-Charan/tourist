Medak Attractions

Medak Cathedral
Medak Cathedral is one of the largest and most beautiful churches in India. Built during the British era, it is known for its Gothic-style architecture and stunning stained-glass windows that depict scenes from the Bible.
Cost to Stay: ₹1,000/night
Edupayala Vana Durga Bhavani Temple
Edupayala Temple is set amidst dense forests and streams, making it a serene pilgrimage spot. The temple becomes the center of attention during the Maha Shivaratri festival when thousands of devotees visit to seek blessings.
Cost to Stay: ₹1,200/night
