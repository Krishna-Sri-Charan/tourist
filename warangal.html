Warangal Attractions

Warangal Fort
Warangal Fort is a historical marvel that dates back to the Kakatiya dynasty. The fort features impressive stone carvings and ancient ruins, making it a top destination for history lovers.
Cost to Stay: ₹1,602/night
Bhadrakali Lake
Bhadrakali Lake is a serene spot for boating and picnics, located near the famous Bhadrakali Temple. The lake offers scenic beauty and a peaceful environment.
Cost to Stay: ₹1,839/night
Pakhal Lake
urrounded by forests, Pakhal Lake is a beautiful man-made lake offering stunning views and boating activities. It's a popular destination for nature lovers.
Cost to Stay: ₹1,400/night
