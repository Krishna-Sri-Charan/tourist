Nirmal Attractions

Kadem Dam
Kadem Dam is a popular weekend destination for locals, offering scenic views and opportunities for boating and picnicking. The dam is surrounded by lush greenery, making it a perfect getaway.
Cost to Stay: ₹1,300/night
Nirmal Paintings Gallery
Nirmal is famous for its unique style of painting, and the gallery showcases some of the best works by local artists. Visitors can also purchase these paintings as souvenirs.
Cost to Stay: ₹2,307/night
