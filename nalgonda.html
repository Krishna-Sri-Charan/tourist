Nalgonda Attractions

Nagarjuna Sagar Dam
Nagarjuna Sagar Dam is one of the largest dams in India, providing a breathtaking view of the Krishna River. The dam also has a viewpoint and nearby parks for visitors to enjoy.
Cost to Stay: ₹2,000/night
Ethipothala Waterfalls
Ethipothala Waterfalls is a beautiful 70-foot cascade of water that merges into a lagoon. The area is surrounded by lush greenery, making it a perfect spot for nature lovers and picnics.
Cost to Stay: ₹1,300/night
Bhongir Fort
Bhongir Fort is a historical fort situated on a massive rock formation. Visitors can trek to the top for panoramic views of the surrounding countryside.
Cost to Stay: ₹2,528/night
