Tirupathi Attractions

Pulicate lake
India’s second-largest brackish water lake, famous for birdwatching.
Cost to Stay: ₹2300 per night
Talakona
A scenic waterfall and a trekking spot amidst dense forests.
Cost to Stay: ₹1000 per night
Chandragiri fort
A historic fort with a rich cultural background, associated with Vijayanagara kings.
Cost to Stay: ₹1000/night
Srikalahasthi temple
It is famous for the Rahu-Ketu pooja, performed to alleviate planetary afflictions.
Cost to Stay: ₹1000/night
Tirupathi
Famous for the Tirumala Venkateswara Temple, a major pilgrimage destination.
Cost to Stay: ₹3000/night
