Jangaon Attractions

Sri Ramalingeswara Swamy Temple (Jeedikal)
This temple, set amidst rocky hills, is a revered pilgrimage site dedicated to Lord Shiva. It’s known for its serene atmosphere and beautiful natural surroundings.
Cost to Stay: ₹1,600/night
Palakurthi Temple
A famous ancient temple dedicated to the poet Palakurthi Somanadha, this temple is surrounded by beautiful natural landscapes, making it a perfect spot for devotees and nature lovers.
Cost to Stay: ₹1,100/night
Pembarthi Village
Pembarthi is renowned for its brass artifacts, making it a must-visit for those interested in local arts and crafts. The artisans of the village have been practicing brasswork for centuries.
Cost to Stay: ₹2,600/night
