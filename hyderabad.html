Adilabad Attractions

Kuntala Waterfalls
The highest waterfall in Telangana, surrounded by dense forests. A popular destination for nature lovers and adventure enthusiasts.
Pochera Waterfalls
A scenic escape with water cascading into a deep pool, ideal for tranquility seekers.
Basar Saraswati Temple
A revered temple along the Godavari River, dedicated to Goddess Saraswati, famous for its architecture.
Kawal Wildlife Sanctuary
A haven for wildlife enthusiasts offering sightings of tigers and leopards, ideal for safaris.
Jainath Temple
An ancient Jain pilgrimage site known for its intricate carvings and historical significance.
