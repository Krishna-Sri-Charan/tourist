Wanaparthy Attractions

Jetprolu Temples
Jetprolu is known for its ancient temples located on the banks of the Krishna River. The temples are significant both historically and religiously.
Cost to Stay: ₹1,516/night
Beechupalli Temple
Beechupalli Temple is a famous temple dedicated to Lord Hanuman, situated on the banks of the Krishna River. The temple is a significant pilgrimage site.
Cost to Stay: ₹1,200/night
