Karimnagar Attractions

Elgandal Fort
Elgandal Fort, located on the banks of the Manair River, is a historic structure known for its robust construction and the beautiful surroundings. The fort also has tombs and a mosque within its premises.
Cost to Stay: ₹1,449/night
Lower Manair Dam
A prominent irrigation project in Telangana, Lower Manair Dam is a peaceful place to visit, with beautiful views of the water reservoir. The dam is particularly lovely during sunrise and sunset.
Cost to Stay: ₹1,200/night
Kondagattu Anjaneya Swamy Temple
One of the most visited temples in the region, this temple dedicated to Lord Hanuman is located amidst picturesque hills and greenery, offering a spiritual experience for devotees.
Cost to Stay: ₹800/night
Ujwala Park
A family-friendly park in the heart of Karimnagar, Ujwala Park offers green spaces, play areas for children, and walking tracks. It is a perfect place for relaxation and outdoor activities.
Cost to Stay: ₹900/night
Rajiv Gandhi Deer Park
Located near Lower Manair Dam, this park is home to a variety of deer species. It’s an excellent spot for wildlife lovers and families looking for a quiet outing surrounded by nature.
Cost to Stay: ₹1,200/night
