Siddipet Attractions

Komuravelli Mallanna Temple
Komuravelli Mallanna Temple is a famous pilgrimage site dedicated to Lord Mallanna (Shiva). Located on a hill, the temple attracts large crowds during the festival of Maha Shivaratri.
Cost to Stay: ₹3,326/night
Gajwel Eco-Park
Gajwel Eco-Park is an eco-friendly park offering scenic beauty, boating, and walking trails. It’s an ideal spot for families looking for a relaxing outing amidst nature.
Cost to Stay: ₹4,775/night
Ranganayaka Sagar Reservoir
Ranganayaka Sagar is a serene reservoir known for its picturesque views. The reservoir is surrounded by lush greenery and is a popular spot for picnics and boating.
Cost to Stay: ₹3,920/night
Wargal Saraswathi Temple
Wargal Saraswathi Temple is a prominent temple dedicated to Goddess Saraswathi, particularly visited by students for blessings before exams.
Cost to Stay: ₹4,775/night
